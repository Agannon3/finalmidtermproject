Stroke

The Three Types of Strokes

A stroke occurs when a blood vessel in the brain ruptures and bleeds
Another cause is a blockage of blood supply to the brain.
There are three main types of strokes
Transient Ischemic Attack
Ischemic Stroke
Hemorrhagic Stroke

Stroke Symptoms

Paralysis
Numbness or Weakness in the arm, face, and leg.
trouble speaking or understanding speech
Confusion
Slurring speech
Trouble Walking
Dizziness
Severe Sudden Headache
