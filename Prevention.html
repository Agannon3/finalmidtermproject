Exercise

Exercise allows for increased blood-flow to the body's circulatory system.
This helps heal the body's blood vessels by breaking down plague caused by lipids.
Exercise also strengthens the muscles of the heart.
Exercise lowers your blood pressure which is good for your circulatory system.

Healthy Diet

This is a no-Brainer!
Eating healthfully will lower your cholesterol and reduce your
risk of developing diabetes.
Most Doctors recommend a diet rich in greens, whole grains, and fruit
to lower your chance of heart disease.
Bill Clinton, who has had quadruple bypass surgery,
is a vegan to lower his risk of cardiovascular complications.

Annual Physical

At an annual physical your doctor will be able to access your short
and long-term risk of developing Heart Disease.
Your Doctor will get your blood-work(lipid panel, sodium levels, glucose)
blood pressure, and heart rate.
Your doctor may also administer an ECG to look for any abnormal findings.
With this information the doctor will be able to make recommendations
and form guidelines to help lower your risk of Heart Disease.
