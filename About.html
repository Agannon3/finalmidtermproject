Why I am interested in Heart Disease

I started learning more about heart disease this year after being
diagnosed with incomplete right bundle branch block.
I remember the nurse telling me "your ecg isn't entirely normal"
and this big wave of uncertainty and depression kicked in.
This combined with COVID isolation made the next three months
of going to the cardiologist and doing tests very difficult.
Thankfully there were no problems!

Sources I used

I got most of my information from the American Heart Association and The Mayo Clinic
Just to warn you, The AHA has done some sketchy publication and health advisory deals
with brands that produce poor quality foods, drinks, and snacks.
Their medical information is accurate but we should be skeptical
of supposed "Heart Healthy" labels on items.
